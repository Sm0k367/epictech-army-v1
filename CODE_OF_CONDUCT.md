# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

**Epic Tech AI Agent Army** is dedicated to making creation open, welcoming, and safe for all—no matter your background, ideas, or skill level.

## Our Standards

- Be friendly, respectful, and welcoming
- Encourage wild creativity and experimentation
- Critique ideas, never people
- No harassment, hate, or discrimination of any kind
- Constructive feedback keeps the project strong

## Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in PRs, issues, or users being blocked at the maintainers' discretion.

---

Creativity is for everyone. We build the future together!
